eSLIME is an upcoming modeling system from Princeton University, designed to make it easier to develop multi-paradigm lattice models and run many instances in parallel.

eSLIME is currently in a pre-alpha state of development: it is not yet ready for use of any kind. In the meantime, you are welcome to follow our progress on Pivotal Tracker (signup required).
